#### About Me

Hi, I'm **Li Li**, a second-year graduate student majoring in software engineering. I love technology and life, and have had two test development internship experiences. I enjoy documenting problems I encounter in study and work. For myself, to fight against forgetting. For others, to be helpful.

#### Postscript

The blog of lili is now open.

In 2019, there is finally a place to write something well.

As a programmer, it would be boring if the wheel of Blog is hung on popular blog programs. I've tried many blogs — CSDN, Jianshu, Open Source China, etc. First, most blog services are too ugly. Second, it's not fun if you can't customize freely. I was too lazy to set things up, so I didn't even have a place to write.

Lu Xun said: *The best time to write a blog is ten years ago, followed by now.*

I hope to keep writing here, whether about technology or life. To borrow a quote from Steve Jobs' Stanford speech:

> *"You can't connect the dots looking forward; you can only connect them looking backwards."*
